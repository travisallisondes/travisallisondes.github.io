Reviewed by Stephan Reinhardt Issue Date 30th Jun 2016 Source 225, The Wine Advocate Drink Date 2023 - 2040
The intensely citrus colored NV Cuvee des Caudalies Grand Cru Extra Brut is 100% old-vines Chardonnay from Avize. (This edition is based on the 2013 vintage, with 50% of the perpetual reserve, and was bottled in April 2014 and disgorged in October 2017.) The wine opens with an intense, concentrated, still yeasty and toasty bouquet with lots of brioche and matured "reserve" aromas. Pure and linear on the palate but also rich and intense, with a charming roundness (dosage of five grams per liter), this is a still terribly young but well-structured and expressive Cuvee des Caudalies, and the finish reveals remarkable salty purity, complexity and mineral tension. I would surely enjoy the 2010 Millesime earlier than this reserve-dominated NV cuvee, as the aging potential should be excellent. Tasted in Reims, April 2018.
