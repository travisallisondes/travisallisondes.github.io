INTRODUCTORY SET-
This toy is ideal for children that are interested in learning the basics of soccer. The set comes complete with a small, introductory rubber ball attached to an adjustable string, a sturdy base, and an air pump to inflate the ball.
WEIGHTED BASE-
To weight the base and keep it securely in place while your child practices their kicking, simply fill the base with water or sand. Your little one can kick the ball as hard as they like, and the base will stay put!
ADJUSTABLE STRING-
The sting that attaches to the ball and returns it back to the player after a kick, is conveniently adjustable up to 42, allowing the set to grow with your child. Simply turn the lever on the back to lengthen or shorten the string.

This soccer training toy can be played with inside or outdoors and is conveniently portable so your child can practice their skills anywhere. Its a great way to encourage physical activity!
